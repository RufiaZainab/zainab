Disadvantages of Misusing Internet

1.Social Isolation:
The excessive use of Internet has brought the issue of isolation of individuals, especially the younger ones, from the mainstream society. The excessive use of Internet badly impacts the psyche and mental capability of individuals. The youths feel less interested in current society. They are becoming more interested in virtual world.
2.The Internet Addiction:
The Internet addiction refers to the excessive use of Internet by individual especially the young people. The internet addiction creates the problems of social isolation and disturbance in relations. The young people have no respect for the society, social values and elders in society.
3.Health Issues:
Our health is associated with not only our internal body mechanism but also our external environment as well. Though you may feel that you are getting a healthy and sufficient diet with no internal complication yet you may feel tired, fatigued and at the loss of mind. It is the fact, that for a good health we need to have a sound exposure to the external world. We can stay healthy, happy and strong only when we are connected with our external world, the beauty of nature. The Internet addiction has forced us to stay on bed and do all the things online. It has cut down our interaction with the external world. It has, thereby, created sever complications with our health as well, like stress, uncertainty, lack of peace and other diseases
4.Online Gaming:
Game addiction has become a global issue today. Through internet million of people play online games together. Many of the games are reported to be very dangerous, like PubG, blue whale etc. Especially, the young adults and children are badly involved in it.
5. Cyber bullying & Frauds:
Previously, it was unthinkable to commit a crime online. Nowadays, with the increasing use of Internet, cyber crime is increasing day by day. Online frauds and deception has badly hit our society. It is again with the misuse of Internet that we are facing these issues today.
7. E commerce and Frauds Associated with it:
E commerce is relatively a new phenomenon in the developing world. Though in developed countries there are laws and regulations to prevent fraud and deception in e commerce business. Unfortunately, the developing countries are still struggling to find ways to prevent e commerce fraud. With the rise of Internet usage, millions of cheap products are being sold online with misleading information. As a result there is great risk to save and secure e business and the trust of people.
8. Sexual Health Issues:
The excessive use of Internet and porn addiction has been pushing our younger generation to the sexual health issues. This addiction has badly impacted the potency and capability of our younger generation.
9. Women Harassment:
Internet has made it possible where people of different groups and different mindset come closer with each other. The instant and unrestricted online interaction has created the issue of harassment of individuals, especially, children, girls and women. Unfortunately, in developing countries there is no strict mechanism to control and punish the potential offenders. As a result the issues of online harassment of women are increasing day by day.
10. Privacy Issues:
Privacy is the right of every individual. Unfortunately, in online world it has become quite hard rather impossible to safeguard one’s privacy. Our smart phones and various other internet services that we use, track down our movements. With the introduction of Artificial intelligence theses machines would be able to intercept and understand our daily life routines. Therefore, there is no privacy in today’s world of technology, due to the excessive use of Internet in our lives.
11.Bombardment of Information
Naturally, the human mind is capable of absorbing a limited amount of information only. With internet, and other medium of communication, we are experiencing the floods of information every where. As a result, our minds are loosing the capability of understanding and judging the things accurately.
12. Stress and Uncertainty Everywhere:
Though human being has become able to resolve most of the life problems easily yet he remains concerned. As it is said, every solution breeds a new problem. Similarly, the problems to today’s world has increased. There is no charm, comfort and the peace of heart in today’s life.
13. Loss of Moral Values:
Morality is the base of any nation. With the misuse of Internet, the moral values of a nation are on decline This can be seen recently in the context of Asian countries where moral values of humanity are under strong attack by anti moral values groups and organizations
14. A Threat to Cultures:
The culture is the identity of a nation. Internet has made it possible to connect easily between the people of different cultures. This cultural diversity, in fact, is hurting the interests of small and weaker cultures. The weak and small cultures are vanishing out quickly.
15. Internet is an Easy Tool of Propaganda:
Propaganda is the spread of misleading information. With the misuse of Internet communication, propaganda is done by groups, organization and other states to target common people as per their plans. Propaganda is on of the deadliest mean of the dangers of today’s world.
16. Spread of Misleading and Harmful Information:
In today’s world, information is everywhere. With the help of Internet and other digital medium there is the flood of information of every type around us. But, with the misuse of Internet, it is getting on quite difficult to separate the truth out of false. The news that we are receiving every second, they are proving to be the unreliable and misleading. The deception has become a common norm of today’s world.
17. Online Blackmailers:
The Internet has become a potential tool for blackmailing. It is very unfortunate to say that, certain individuals or groups misuse Internet. By Internet the blackmailers can misuse your information and present it in a wrong way to get us trapped. Young adults are the potential target of online blackmailing and harassment. Though in developed countries there are laws to prevent and punish that crime but the developing countries are yet to get ready to face these challenges.
18. Inferiority Complex:
The Inferiority complex refers to the feelings of loss of respect and confidence by an individual or nation. Internet is a fancy world. The things are portrayed beyond reality. There is a lot of deception and fraud over Internet. By believing over the fancy world of Internet, where nothing is completely true, the individuals or nations feel themselves less respected and less valued. This complex then restricts a nation’s way towards success.
19. The Generation Gap:
Though Internet is very beneficial for studies yet there are a few concerns regarding its demerits for study. The chief concern is the misuse of Internet by students. They get deeply involved in Internet. As a result, they waste their time in immoral activities online like, gaming, internet addiction, etc. The excessive and negative use of Internet is harmful for studies.
21. Pornography:
Pornography is a heinous crime. Unfortunately, young adult are deeply involved in porn addiction. Watching porn has sever impacts on mentality and physical ability of young adults and students. With spread of Internet and smart phone technology this menace is spreading out very quickly.
22. Defamation and Loss of Personal Respect:
Internet and social media are a great sensation. On Internet it has become very easy to make hero a villain and the villain a hero. Social media is a potential tool to degrade and harm any individual or organization. It greatly impacts the worth and dignity of individuals.
23. Impacts on Children:
The misuses of Internet has bad impacts on Children. They are exposed to objectionable material. It impacts the psyche of children badly. Therefore, only the regular monitoring of Internet usage of children by parents, can only make them secure.
24. Hacking of Data & Communication by Hackers:
Hacking is an unethical way of stealing the data and resources of other people, organizations and state for various hidden uses. Hacking is one of the most dangerous threat of Internet
25. The attacks on National Security & Sovereignty
National security is the criterion of a safe and secure nation. The misuse of Internet has created a lot of hurdles in the way of managing security. Nowadays, the thieves are well advanced and very professional. They misuse Internet for planning and executing their aims and strategies. Internet is therefore a double edged sword.
26. Spread of Terrorism via Internet:
Terrorism is a global issue. Unfortunately, the terrorist are using Internet to plan their attacks. They misuse Internet for their gains. It has become a great ever challenge to the states and organizations to curb the menace of terrorism
27. Deceptive Propaganda and Persuasion:
Propaganda and Persuasion are the tools of gaining public opinion for some intended goals. By misusing Internet Propaganda is done to mislead the public opinion for some ulterior goals.
28. Violation of Human Rights:
Internet is greatly misused for the violation of human rights. It is used to attack privacy, steal data and misuse the same, etc.
29. Online cheating and Fraud:
Cheating and fraud in online business has become the order of day. Through Internet people are lured into and in returns, fraud and deception is done with them.
30. Cheap Advertising of Sub Standard Products:*
Online advertising has become very common. Thorough various social media platforms, the black mailers do the fraud with innocent people by making them believe in bad products. There is no serious and standard advertising. Through constant advertising people are lured via multiple scam programs to buy the products that prove sub standard and of cheap value.
